2 Description of Service

VizBuddy is a Chrome browser extension that captures a screenshot of your active browser tab and sends it to a third-party AI service (Anthropic's Claude API) to generate an analyst-style summary of the visual content on screen. The service includes:

  • Structured analysis output — Headline, Summary, Watch Out For, and Confidence rating
  • Compose This — a feature that transforms analysis into a ready-to-send message in multiple tones
  • Analysis history — session-based for free users, 30-day persistent for Pro users

VizBuddy requires an active internet connection and a compatible version of Google Chrome to function.

3 Free and Pro Tiers
