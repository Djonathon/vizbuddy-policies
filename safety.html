VizBuddy Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 4, 2025

VizBuddy is a Chrome extension that captures screenshots of browser tabs and analyzes them using AI. This policy explains what data is involved and how it is handled.

What VizBuddy does

When you click "Capture Full Page," VizBuddy takes a screenshot of your currently active browser tab and sends it to the Anthropic API to generate an analysis. The result is displayed in the side panel.

Data sent to Anthropic

Screenshots are transmitted to Anthropic's API solely to generate analysis text. VizBuddy does not store, log, or share these images. Anthropic's handling of API data is governed by their own privacy policy: anthropic.com/privacy.

Data stored locally

VizBuddy stores the following on your device only, using Chrome's local storage:

  • Your last 3 analysis results (text only, no screenshots)
  • A daily usage counter that resets at midnight

This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to VizBuddy or any third party.

Data we do not collect

VizBuddy does not collect, transmit, or store any personally identifiable information, browsing history, account credentials, or any data beyond what is described above.
